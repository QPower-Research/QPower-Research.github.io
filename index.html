About

Find Out More About Us

QPower is a Quantum Research Community that was formed under the Microsoft Student Partner Program by Syed Farhan in 2019.

We are a group of researchers/students, aiming for a research driven community in Quantum Computing.


Here are some of our goals

  • Contributing our bit in the form of Publications.

    By learning and realizing about the developments of Quantum in the present day, we aim to contributing towards the future of Quantum Computing.

  • Forming an international community of quantum minds.

    We are actively collaborating and learning along with people of Quantum. We aim to develop a strong international network to promote effient learning, research and growth as a single entity.

We are constantly looking for collaborations and contributions from Universities and Industries working on Quantum Technology.
